## 1. What is a log?
A log is a record of events generated by systems, applications, or devices to track activities and errors.

---

## 2. What is SIEM?
SIEM (Security Information and Event Management) collects, analyzes, and correlates security logs to detect threats.

---

## 3. Why logs are important?
Logs help detect attacks, troubleshoot issues, ensure compliance, and support forensic investigations.

---

## 4. What is anomaly detection?
Anomaly detection identifies unusual patterns that differ from normal system behavior.

---

## 5. Examples of security logs?
- Authentication logs
- Firewall logs
- System logs
- IDS/IPS logs
